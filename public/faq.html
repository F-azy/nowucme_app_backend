3. How does the nearby discovery work?

When "Discover Mode" is enabled in the app:

  • Your encrypted location is sent to our server.
  • You can see users who are within 50 meters and have also enabled discover mode.
  • You can instantly view their social media profiles.
  • Once you turn off discover mode, your location stops being shared.
